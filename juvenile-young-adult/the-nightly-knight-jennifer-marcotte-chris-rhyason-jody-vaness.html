Climb the Cliff of Doom with the blue-eyed Knight and seek out the beast behind the bedroom door! Open your eyes and imagination to enchanted worlds, dragon lairs, and a battle beyond the ages, beyond the words of storybook pages. Prepare yourself for the battle the Battle of Bed Time! The eyes of children tell a million stories. Every full hearted laugh turns another page, each tear that falls fills a thousand paragraphs. Footnotes follow every moment of fear, and smiles speak sentences. These are moments in time, captured in a story. Each story has a name, each personality fills its pages. The kids are real, their lives are her muse; their quirks are her casts, their challenges are her treasures. As her own children grown, Jennifer marvels in the wonder of their dreams and encourages their curiosity. She finds more wisdom in their words than in all of the classrooms of the world. Every day, she gives gratitude for her children, for your children, and for innocence.
