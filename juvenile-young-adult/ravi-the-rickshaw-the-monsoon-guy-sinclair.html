This is the story of how a loveable young auto rickshaw named Ravi learns about the monsoons of India, from his very first customer to his meeting of Sri the truck. Ravi leaves the factory for the first time with all the other rickshaws as they go off in different directions, discovering and experiencing the sights and sounds of their native country.This book was created with the intention to teach children about India and its culture, which is not always portrayed in the media. This is an ideal book for any children between the ages of 4-10 as it can be read by children or to them as a unique bedtime story. Experience and enjoy Ravi's adventures as he learns his way around incredible India!About the Author:Guy Sinclair is one of the most experienced and respected producers in the Middle East with over 25 years in the film industry working across features, television, TV Commercials, and documentaries.He is the proud father of three girls and whenever he had the chance, he would read them magical bedtime stories from around the world.One of his many film assignments took him to India where he was entranced by the infusion of culture and colours that the historical country offered.Guy wanted to share these experiences with his children and realized that a great way to do this was through the adventures of a young, child-like character. So Ravi and his friend Sri were created and the story began.
