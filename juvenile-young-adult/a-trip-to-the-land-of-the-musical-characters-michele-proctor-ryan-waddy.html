A Trip to the Land of the Musical Characters is a fun filled book; full of imagination about a little girl named Sara who saved a magical land from deterring from a mischievous bee. Sara Zimmerman is a small girl with a lot of heart. She had enough heart to bring down a giant bee and restore The Land of the Musical Characters. After entering this land from playing a keyboard and going into a slight trance she meets musical characters that take her on a journey that she would never forget. I wrote this book because I had an encounter with musical beings when I took keyboarding in school. I wanted to share them with the world. Many small children have seen them too.
