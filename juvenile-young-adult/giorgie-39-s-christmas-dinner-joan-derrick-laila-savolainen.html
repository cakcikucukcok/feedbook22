Giorgie’s Christmas Dinner is a sensitive approach to nature’s way. In the second of the series, author Joan Derrick introduces us to Giorgie’s guide, Astara, and her intuitive voice, which shows us how everything in nature gives a gift of itself to another.

What is unique about Joan’s book is that she brings simple wisdom to the understanding that everything is connected. By encouraging young people to tap into the energy and gifts around us, Joan empowers children to clearly see the web that holds us together, and that everything has its place and purpose. Giorgie’s Christmas Dinner represents the next generation of children’s books and is a special gift to parents, whose busy lives, hopes and aspirations for their children make Joan’s offering an essential tool.
