In writing her first childrens book, The Orange Barnge, author Wilma Ruggiero introduced her young readers to the magic of owning a farm as well as having created a word that rhymes with orange. She expands upon the world of farm life with this follow-up book, Adventures at the Orange Barnge. Her main character, Tannon Fortuna, fulfills his ambition of owning a farm. His love of nature, animals, and country living, shines through in this delightful read. A barn, cows, chickens, cats, dogs, and a humorous turkey all come to life. The magical, mystical, and musical happenings provide the young reader with the promise of dreaming.
