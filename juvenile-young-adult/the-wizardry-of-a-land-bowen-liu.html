Seritol is only a little boy studying the art of spells until a messenger from war comes. He announces that the young boys father has fallen and as the messenger speaks the words that Seritols parent wants him to pass, confidence grows within him.

Now with Seritols staff and his future trusted team, he goes on to embark on an epic quest to save the means of the towns people and to defeat the dark wizard.
