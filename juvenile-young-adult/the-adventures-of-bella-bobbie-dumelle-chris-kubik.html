Bella and the rest of her bird friends and family live in a large shade tree behind a bakery on a busy street in Chicago. Not only do they enjoy the view from their high perch, but they appreciate the bakery treats Dennis and his sister, Katie, toss out the backdoor. Cinnamon rolls are a favorite. Bella tries to live in peace and harmony with every creature, but it is not always easy, especially when Sonny the squirrel tries to steal all the bakery goodies and hoard them for himself. Thats when the trouble, and the fun, begins. An adventurous bird, Bella loves to experience new things. In The Adventures of Bella, she arranges a trip to the zoo where the bird friends are introduced to a variety of animals. Bella meets Owen the owl who tells her about the seven continents. She wants to visit the all. But thats a story for next time.
