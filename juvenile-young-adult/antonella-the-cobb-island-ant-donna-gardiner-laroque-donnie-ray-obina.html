Antonella the Cobb Island Ant is about a little ant that travels to Cobb Islands Maryland, a true location on the Potomac River. The inspiration for this story comes from listening to the residents of Cobb islands complaints of their yearly springtime return of their kitchen countertop ants. Cobb Island is a tourist resort area and consists of long time residents.
