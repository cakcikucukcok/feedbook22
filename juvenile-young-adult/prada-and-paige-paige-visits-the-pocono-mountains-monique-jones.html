Prada and Paige are Yorkshire terriers that were born in the Bronx in New York City. Paige moved to Manhattan, New York, with Kayla, and Prada moved to the Pocono Mountains of Pennsylvania with Mama Moe. Paige is excited when she learns she's going to the scenic Pocono Mountains to visit her older brother Prada. When Paige leaves the hustle and bustle and bright lights of New York City, she's surprised to learn of a whole new world, one that contains marvelous views of nature and creatures she's never seen. Prada introduces Paige to his friends in the country including Theo the deer, Alice the woodpecker, and Tanya the owl. A picture book for children, Paige and Prada, by author Monique Jones, offers a view of the beautiful Pocono Mountains through the eyes of two charming dogs.
