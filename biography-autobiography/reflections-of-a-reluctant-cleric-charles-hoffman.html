Many who espouse Christian or other religious faiths assume that they are insulated from the "slings and arrows of outrageous fortune." How often do we hear the phrase, "What did I do to deserve this?" There are even some purveyors of faith who suggest that faith somehow offers a shield from the dark side of life. There are those who maintain that faith leads to health, happiness, prosperity, and security. And while there may be some truth in some instances, it is never an inevitable outcome.
