Matteo guides us through his world after almost 12 years of silence. His trapped intelligence released, he shares amazing insights on love, God, finding happiness and the gifts of autism. Through a miraculous discovery, Matteo’s mother was finally able to communicate with her son, exposing a well of intelligence and heart way beyond her imagination. Here, Matteo’s first book, is the light and culmination of locked up thoughts and messages. His messages are enlightening to say the least, about life, love, autism, and God. This book is filled with laughter and joy written letter by letter on a letter board straight from Matteo's heart to the reader. He gives incredible insight and tips on raising and befriending an autistic child or autistic adult. One of his favorite messages is of love and the most insightful message is: Assume intelligence.
