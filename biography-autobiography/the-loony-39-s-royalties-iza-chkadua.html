The collection consists of twenty-five stories that are mostly based upon real facts that happened in Georgia in the 20th and 21st centuries and represent our customs and traditions. I think the characters and subjects of the stories will be interesting for foreign readers.

Anyone interested in literature can read the book and no matter whether hes 13 or very, very old.

The collection is a mixture of philosophy and mysticism.

Love and hatred, treachery and devotion, consideration and indifference are the things that people are mostly inclined to think about and the author is not an exception. All the characters have something in common with the author and are prototypes of the people famous for the so-called Rose Revolution. But there can be no revolution without changes in ones mind.
