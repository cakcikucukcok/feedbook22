Little children in the hospitals lay in cots looking out between bars, doing hard time as days passed without a nappy change, a hug, or any sort of encouragement to learn the basics of walking and talking. The babies would turn up at the hospital perfectly healthy and slowly, but surely, become disabled, both mentally and physically. Sue's empathy with the children's plight was real and palpable and what was so impressive was that she extended that empathy toward the parents who had abandoned their children. - Jan Bowron, Journalist

TEARS AND TRIUMPHS: Follow Sue van Schreven's inspiring and fast-moving story. This New Zealander is sure to capture your heart as she sets out to rescue some of the world's abandoned and forgotten. Sue's personal journey and how she established the valuable charitable work of Orphans Aid International is a must-read. Sue is an experienced and dynamic speaker who has been involved in charitable and volunteer work for almost twenty-five years. Visit www.suesblog.co for regular news.

Orphans Aid International rescues children from abusive and unsafe situations and places them in loving families or homes. Join this journey to alleviate suffering from the world's most vulnerable. www.orphansaidinternational.org
