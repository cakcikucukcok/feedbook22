Here is the history of carols, Christmas Cards, and the tradition of ‘first footing’ on New Year’s Day. The chapters, written with the imaginative delight of a poet, explore the traditions of Christmas in England since its earliest times, ranging from Christmas Eve to Twelfth Night.<br><br>This short work, which also contains beautiful illustrations by Robin Jaques and Joan Hassall, is a charming companion to anyone interested in the magic and tradition inherent in the Christmas Season.<br><br>“A lovely benevolence; the author’s knowledge, his sense of values, his breadth of outlook are in evidence on every page.” <i>John O’London’s Weekly</i><br><br>“Possessing enchantment of matter, it has also enchantment of manner.” <i>Time and Tide</i><br><br>
