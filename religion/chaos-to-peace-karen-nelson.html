You don't have to be overwhelmed at the state of your house anymore, because help is here! CHAOS to Peace is a 31-day devotional designed to encourage you and strengthen your faith as you complete the 31 BabySteps of the FlyLady housekeeping routine. Includes a foreword by FlyLady, Marla Cilley.

This book is for you if you struggle to change lifelong habits of CHAOS, you need hope to see the end goal of your BabySteps, or you’re overwhelmed and need the courage to keep moving forward. 

The secret to peace in the midst of CHAOS (can't have anyone over syndrome) is your strength in God. Through carefully selected biblical examples of others who have gone before us, FlyLady mentor Karen Nelson lays out the truth of what’s possible when you trust in the Lord. You’ll learn how to make your home a sanctuary and your mindset one of peace as you walk out each step with Jesus. Buoyed by the compassion and understanding in these pages, you’ll feel refreshed and encouraged each day. And know this: You CAN transform your life and home. You ARE ready to conquer the CHAOS!

“Stepping in faith each day gives us the strength to overcome the CHAOS in our lives. Karen has taken the FlyLady System and paired it with the biblical teachings to give you a steadfast foundation for your life and home. May God continue to bless her!” ~ Marla Cilley, the FlyLady, www.flylady.com, author of Sink Reflections and CHAOS to Clean
