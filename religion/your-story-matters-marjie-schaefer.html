The Lord has preserved for us a record that is filled with stories and

examples so that we can learn from Him and how He worked in the

lives of others. His Word is living and active and able to change us.

The best thing we can do to experience His transforming power is

to dig into His Word for ourselves and to learn from those who

have gone before us.

This study has us examining two Old Testament stories of Habakkuk,

the prophet, and of David, the shepherd, warrior king. Both of

these stories communicate life-changing principles for us today.

This study will equip us to cultivate daily habits of declaring the

truth of God’s Word out loud, along with deliberate times of worship

and praise.

You will discover that because it fits within God’s great Kingdom story!
