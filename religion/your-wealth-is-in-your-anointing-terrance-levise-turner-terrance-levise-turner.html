Your Wealth Is In Your Anointing will inspire the reader to enjoy the benefits of being connected with the Source of all life, joy, prosperity, and fulfillment, the Lord Jesus Christ!

• discover your wealth potential and keys to financial security through Christ.

• pass on this knowledge to help your children have a brighter future.

• realize the practical value of your connection with Jesus Christ for your future and life purpose.
