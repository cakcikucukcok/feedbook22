The lack of a good foundation, education and guidance before and during dating are some of the causes of divorce even among Christians. Unfortunately, not many resources are provided to help Christian singles to become knowledgeable when they commence dating. Hence, they go into it blindly.

This book, Dating, Sex and Cohabitation, is written to provide the reader with relevant information to carry him or her successfully through the potential minefield of relationship. It brings the reader’s attention to the “Small Print” which he/she is likely to overlook when dating and or considering marriage.

Pre-marital sex is discussed at length and the book equips you with how to set boundaries and to stay off pre-marital sex for reasons that are well explained. This book also discusses in-depth the issue of cohabitation and draws attention to the many negative effects this might have in your relationship, both now and in the future.

Valuable biblical advice, guidance and information on dating and courtship which will prove very beneficial to the reader as he or she progresses in the relationship are also provided.
