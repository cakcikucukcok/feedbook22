“It’s great being here on Sunday morning, but how do I get more God into my life during the week?” 

That was the question asked of the author by a friend after worship service one Sunday morning. She shares, "Fueled perhaps by too much caffeine earlier in the day, though hopefully Spirit led and inspired, I found myself with notebook and pen in hand at 12:23am, writing a letter about what had worked for me in my own faith journey. Whether what I did would work for someone else, I didn’t know. I just knew I had to write it down before I could sleep."  The 10 Things is the essence of that letter, plus a good bit more. It is an invitation to dive more deeply into your walk of faith. Mimi shares with honesty and a touch of humor her own experiences in seeking a closer walk with God, finding her way from a traditional Lutheran experience to an Evangelical Christian church.

Mimi Schlichter is an artist, author, and musician. She is passionate about faith, family, and friends. More information at MimisArt.com. Blog at TheSacredThings.com.
