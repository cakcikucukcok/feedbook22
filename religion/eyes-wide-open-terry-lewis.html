Are your spiritual eyes wide open so they can see the focus of your heart?

Vision – the ability to see – is truly a precious gift.   From a spiritual perspective, vision is the focus of our heart.  It determines the direction of our lives, and ultimately, the success of our faith walk.

In “Eyes Wide Open,” Pastor Terry Lewis explores seven powerful directives from Paul’s first letter to Thessalonica specifically designed to expand the field of vision for the Christ-follower, walking with clear focus to avoid temptation and daily spiritual victory.  

“In Eyes Wide Open, Pastor Terry Lewis provides applicable Biblical truths to instruct and inspire the reader in their spiritual journey.  He not only identifies many of the spiritual challenges believers face but also provides positive action steps to enable spiritual growth and victory.  This book will encourage you and provide a foundation for greater spiritual victories in your life and ministry.”                                                                                                                                                                                  James E. Rayburn, D.Min., Assistant General Bishop - Pentecostal Church of God

Terry Lewis is the senior pastor of FaithPointe Ministries in Lexington, Kentucky. He and his wife, Tresa, have ministered with passion and humility as pastors, evangelists, and teachers for nearly 30 years.

Pastor Terry is a native of Morgan County, Kentucky, and is a graduate of Morehead State University and Life Christian University. He currently serves as Director of Ministry Development for the Pentecostal Church of God, Kentucky District.
