Pure God addresses areas where Christians and non-Christians often have uncertain beliefs. If left unattended, such beliefs become stumbling blocks for unbelievers and create doubt and confusion in the minds of believers.

The subject matter delves into controversial areas of eternal security, the deity of Jesus, the Holy Trinity, baptism in the Holy Spirit, speaking in tongues, and prophesying. Pure God provides insightfound in no other bookabout the origin of the people of Nod and the identity of the Nephilim. Moreover, readers learn what the Bible teaches about faith, sin, salvation, evil, death, and the end times.

Pure God is more than a simple question-and-answer book. Ensuing topics, appurtenant passages of scripture, and true-to-life illustrations progressively unravel the mystery of God. Each chapter draws the reader closer to the Lord Jesus Christ. Readers experience God's irresistible love and discover what it means to be a Christian.
