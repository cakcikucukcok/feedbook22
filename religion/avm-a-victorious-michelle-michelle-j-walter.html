This is a fast-paced and deceptively simple testimony of faith in Gods grace and forgiveness that is a must-read for anyone who thinks they are beyond the bounds of grace or who has difficulty in seeing Gods goodness in the midst of trouble. Reflecting back on her life, Michelle shares an intimate picture of her coming of age, teen-age marriage, abortions, remarriage, motherhood, divorce and the brain surgery that changed everything. With humor and unapologetic honesty the reader can easily see the hand of God at work, guiding, correcting, holding, His daughter as she travels from simple belief toward full surrender to Him. It is a story full of love for family and friends and an expression of joy and deep gratitude for the life she now lives in the Lord. What a good read. Let the journey continue!
