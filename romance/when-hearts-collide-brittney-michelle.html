Londyn is a 21-year-old young woman with a guarded heart, and a personality loved by many. Solo is a charming ladies' man striving to become more than a statistic. In this coming-of-age story, the young college couple find themselves on a collision course to love. As they work towards building a future together, they must face realities of the past.
