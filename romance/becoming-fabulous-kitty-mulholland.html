“The thing is, I just don’t find you attractive any more.”

It’s the last thing in the world that Leah Bennett expected to hear from her husband of seven years.

It’s now January. Said husband has moved out and Leah is alone. While pondering what to do with her new-found freedom, a passing reference to the swinging scene in an erotic novel is about to change her life forever.

Seeking greater and more daring heights of sexual pleasure provides only temporary relief to her anguish and she decides to take a break from it all to recharge her batteries and rethink her life. 

She can’t stay away for long though, and soon finds herself drawn back to her fabulous group of friends.

Will she ever find her soulmate amongst the hedonistic and mysterious world of sexual misfits?
