Married for His One-Night Heir by Jennifer Hayward

Commanding Santo Di Fiore is stunned to see Giovanna Castiglione again. Her unexpected appearance at his luxurious party in the Bahamas leaves him craving answers. But can this be a marriage in name only, or will their red-hot chemistry take over?
