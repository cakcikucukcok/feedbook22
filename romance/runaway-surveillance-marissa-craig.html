Victory City, a place where crime is welcomed so long as it‘s recorded.  A place where post college runaway, Natsumi, can decide what part she plays in the world after declaring known criminal, Hayate, her guardian angel and intense detective, Onaga, her boyfriend. 

Hayate finds Natsumi inspecting camera parts at her new job and offers her a chance to stop running away and leverage the city’s surveillance to see who’s really committing the crimes.  He wants to show her how detectives pool blood to gain rank and prepare her mind for an unwelcomed guest.  Onaga has to work fast to show her that Hayate‘s no angel but she won‘t listen if he‘s no better. 

It’s hard for Natsumi to make up her mind when she‘s falling for both of them.  Should she be with Hayate or Onaga?  Should she work to protect her new city or overturn it?  Natsumi continues to be afraid of the sound of her own voice.  Especially when she sees how commanding it can be.

Follow the story through the perspectives of all the characters.
