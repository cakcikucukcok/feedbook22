While reading through several short stories, you will come across a series of emotions, highlights, and communication differences that will set these tales apart from others, still allowing you to bask of the generous light of the genre you are attached to. Such highlights and happenings include things like:

- Arguing, resisting, kissing, and making up.

- Marriage proposals, and the entire game leading towards that special moment.

- Princesses in castles who don’t feel like they get what they deserve.

- Vampires and their peculiar dealings with actual human beings.

- Perspectives from males and females who experience the same occurrence but feel differently about it.

- Cross-cultural relationships and particular feelings attached to them.

If you can’t wait to be entertained and put on your pink glasses to the world of romance short-reads, then I invite you to start reading as quickly as possible.
