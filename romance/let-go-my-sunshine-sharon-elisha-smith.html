These two women have been best friends ever since childhood. Theyve shared some of the same thoughts for the future, gone to the same schools, lived on the same block, wore each others clothing and copied hairstyles. Through the ups and downs, they have remained friends. But, when Kiome enters the picture, will this friendship last? Sheena cant seem to understand why it is that men are drawn to her best friend Charly. Sheena has the potential to attract the right kind of guy given the opportunity.
