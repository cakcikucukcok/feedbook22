This work offers a summary of the book “GREAT COMMUNICATION SECRETS OF GREAT LEADERS” by John Baldoni.John Baldoni is a management communications and leadership consultant. He has more than 20 years of experience consulting in this area to a number of companies in a variety of industries.To be effective as a leader, you need to be a good communicator. This, however, is more than simply being a dynamic speaker. Good leaders intuitively understand communication has to be a two-way street in order to be effective. Not only do leaders have to become good at public speaking but they also have to enhance their ability to listen for feedback and then respond appropriately. As this is done over multiple iterations, whatever the leader is attempting to communicate will come into sharper focus for everyone involved.In Great Communication Secrets Of Great Leaders, John Baldoni distills the communication techniques of today's greatest leaders into step-by-step solutions. This excellent guide offers sound advice and useful strategies to enhance your value as a leader.
