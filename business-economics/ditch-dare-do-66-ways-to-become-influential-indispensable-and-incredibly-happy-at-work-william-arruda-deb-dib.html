Personal branding is today's killer app and must-have skill for career-minded leaders, executives, teams, and companies. It's the definitive (and irreverent) career and corporate success plan for defining and living the power of brand--with an action plan that can be executed in 9 minutes a day! Anyone who follows this proven "GPS for success" will advance faster, support the corporate brand, deliver value to their companies--and become influential, indispensable, and incredibly happy at work!
