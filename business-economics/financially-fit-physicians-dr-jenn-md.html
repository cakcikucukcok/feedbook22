Medical school is expensive, frequently leaving its graduates struggling with debt for years. Physicians dedicate their lives to learning their craft. However, that knowledge does not always include a financial education. That is where Dr. Jenn comes in. 

Dr. Jennifer Miles-Thomas has been there. When she finished residency and started her first attending job, she found she was ill-prepared to handle the new complexities of her financial situation and fell heavily into debt. Now, debt-free and financially fit, she shares her hard-earned knowledge with her fellow physicians and other high-income professionals, breaking down complex concepts into actionable items. In Financially Fit Physicians, she talks about strategies for getting out of debt, paying off student loans, choosing the right insurance, planning for retirement, and more. 

It’s time to gain financial freedom! Read this book and learn how to become financially fit!
