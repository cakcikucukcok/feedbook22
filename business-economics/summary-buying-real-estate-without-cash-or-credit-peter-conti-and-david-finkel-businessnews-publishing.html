This work offers a summary of the book “BUYING REAL ESTATE WITHOUT CASH OR CREDIT” by Peter Conti and David Finkel.

Peter Conti is an ex-auto mechanic turned real estate investor. David Finkel is an ex-Olympic-level athlete who also turned real estate investor. They have co-developed fifteen real estate investment courses which have trained in excess of 100,000 students resulting in more than $1 billion of real estate sales.

The real estate investment field is still wide open, and anyone who wants to can still make good money by buying and selling real estate. In Buying Real Estate without Cash or Credit, the authors present the five core skills of real estate investment success. According to them, once you master these five skills, you have everything you need to get started today making money investing in real estate. To reap the big rewards, however, it’s not enough just to know what these skills are. You also have to apply them in your own real estate investment activities day-by-day and week-by week.

If you are interested in real estate investing, Buying Real Estate without Cash or Credit is a book for you. Conti and Finkel demonstrate you don’t need cash or credit to succeed in this field.
