    REASONS TO BUY THIS NEW EDITION:

  • Explains all the considerations and tasks involved in creating a nonprofit organization, whether it is just coming together for the first time, or has already informally been in operation for a while. The book provides comprehensive, big-picture information while also outlining detailed legal and practical requirements.
  • Helps newbies to the nonprofit world understand the legal structure of nonprofits and crucial management tasks including how to build a powerful board, engage in effective planning, and raise money for the organization so it can achieve its mission. It also clarifies the difference between the state nonprofit corporation structure and federal tax-exempt status, which is not well understood by many new to the world of nonprofits.
  • Is written for people who are passionate about a cause, but may not know the first thing about the legal structure of nonprofits or how to manage them. Besides offering detailed legal and management information, the book addresses the realities faced by many activists who may have been pursuing their cause for years without tackling the formalities.
  • The 8th edition will include updated strategies for maximizing your nonprofit's effectiveness online, including boosting engagement with your supporters, populations served, and donors. It will offer the latest information on social media best practices, trends in crowdfunding, email outreach strategy and more. The new edition will also provide updates on alternative legal structures such as benefit corporations, including recent state adoptions. It will also offer the latest strategies for using systems and technology to manage operations, such as managing donor or member lists, staff, and finances.
