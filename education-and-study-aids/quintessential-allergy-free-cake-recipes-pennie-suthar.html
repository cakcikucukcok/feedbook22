A diagnosis of multiple allergies or intolerances can be the beginning of a scary journey. But once you discover the possible food alternatives, it becomes a gastronomic adventure. This Quintessential Allergy Free Cookbook is comprised of information gained during fourteen years of allergy free cooking, development and experimentation in a home and commercial environment. It is designed to help you find your way through the tricky maze that is allergy free cooking with multiple food allergies and intolerances.

This book is a how-to guide on food alternatives, where to buy them, and how to use these unfamiliar ingredients in every day allergy free recipes. It is a collection of cooking tips, recipes, and insightful information which includes a range of allergy free tasks like making dairy free milks, making your own gluten free flour, dealing with new or unfamiliar ingredients, and adapting favourite recipes to allergy free. You will also learn basic cookery and how to troubleshoot gluten and egg free baking, how to create raw cakes and bean cakes and so much more. It will demystify your allergy free journey and give you independence by empowering you with the skills, information, and resources needed to prepare delicious allergy free treats, with ease, in your own kitchen.

Beautiful, full colour, close-up photographs accompany more than fifty-five nutritious and delicious cake recipes in a clear concise layout with allergen dots to guide you along with additional cooking tips. Printed in large font helps you to read it with ease, even in a dimly lit spaces. The recipes are free from up to eleven main allergens: wheat, gluten, dairy, cane-sugar, egg, lactose, fructose, soy, yeast, peanuts, tree nuts, and corn. Lastly, a section on Icings, Topping and Decorating with allergy-free ingredients is included, enabling you to create endless delicacies. Enjoy!
