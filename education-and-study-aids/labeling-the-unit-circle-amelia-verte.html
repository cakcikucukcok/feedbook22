Calculus is a hard subject, so I have created this ebook to help math students with one of the most important tool in Calculus: the unit circle. Anyone who takes Calculus or who will take it in the future will need to master the degree and radian angle measure conversion because the radian angle measure is more dominant in Calculus than the degree angle measure. If you think you only need to know the degree angle measure in the unit circle, I advise you to think again! All Calculus concepts are built on top of one another. In order to pass Calculus, you will have to fully understand all - if not most - of the concepts! That is why I created this ebook: to help math students who take or who will take Calculus master the labeling of the degree and radian angle measure in the unit circle. This ebook is very helpful, and please spread the word about this ebook to help your fellow family, friends, and classmates! Thank you, and I wish you a great math success!!!!!
