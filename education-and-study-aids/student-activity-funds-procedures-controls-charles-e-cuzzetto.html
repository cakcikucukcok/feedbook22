This book helps school business officials establish procedures for tracking and controlling student activity funds at the school and district levels. The book also provides an assessment framework for students, activity sponsors, or administrative staff who are performing internal audits. Sample forms and worksheets in every chapter make this practical text a true 'how-to' guide.
