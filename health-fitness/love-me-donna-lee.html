Love Me

Ive written this special little book as a place that will guide your feminine self to be more confident and to live an empowered life.

Were living in a world driven by business, and its often difficult to slow down to love yourself and nurture your own needs.

Struggling with anorexia and depression, I was slowly recovering to repair and restore my brokenness, and I am sharing what I now know to you.

If you have forgotten how truly awesome you are, then the words contained in this book will help you remember.

All that is required is that you tell yourself how amazing you are, and to help you out, I have placed in my book a short heartfelt story and positive affirmations you can say to yourself each day.

If you want to be confident in your own skin, to feel more alive, then talk to yourself with love and understanding.

It doesnt cost anything to say nice things about yourself, and your body, mind, heart, and spirit will heal as a result of you spending a few minutes each day speaking with compassion and kindness.

If you do this, new doors will open up for you, new possibilities will arise, and you will discover whatever you need is already within you.

I am nurturing my inner child. She frequently wants to come out and play, and when she does, I love her and her childlike ways. She is beautiful, and she teaches me what I need to know.

You have ancient knowledge of wisdom inside of you, and your heart is ready to hear your soft and gentle voice.

I encourage you to spend as little as twenty minutes a day, expressing my powerful words to ignite the little child within.

Know that you are loved.
