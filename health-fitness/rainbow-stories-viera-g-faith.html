This book helps with raising your child with the knowledge of their RAINBOW NUMBERS & COMMUNICATION and thus develops the childs personality and prevents conflicts.

To read this story choose a calm evening and before sleep with minimal distractions. The picture part of this book faces the child so that the child will fully perceive the illustrations while at the same time listening to the stories, therefore better imagine the situation. According to instruction, in the first part of the book the child should allocate the two fir cones to the tree that they view as being most important in the whole forest. After reading the story, leave your sweetheart to settle down while you study the second half of the book. Here you will find the knowledge of RAINBOW NUMBERS & COMMUNICATION . I believe that this knowledge will help you to make a beautiful relationship with your most dearest.

With love, Viera G. Faith
