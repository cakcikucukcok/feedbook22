     This self-help trade book teaches readers how to successfully learn their lessons in life. Complete with 45 workbook-type activities, it helps readers discover the unique lessons that are important to them and apply those in their lives.

     The book is based on the premise that we’re all here to learn certain lessons. By doing that, we naturally accomplish our goals, achieve our dreams, and fulfill our purpose in life. 

     That’s because regardless of where we’re starting from, life lessons can make our lives better. Much better. They provide the “practical wisdom” that accelerates our learning and helps us reach our goals faster and easier.

     So if you’re struggling just to get by or have some nagging problems, life lessons can help.  If you’re doing okay but want to do better, life lessons can teach you how.  Even if you’re already wildly successful, you can use the information from your life lessons to find even more success. 

     The author taught the material in this book to thousands of university students; it was refined based on their input. Major topics include basic life skills such as time management, procrastination, stress management, and goal setting. It is appropriate for men and women, adolescents, and adults.

     Additional topics cover what are called “hazardous zones”—the ways that people get often get stuck in their lives like fear, limiting beliefs, addictions, and excessive worrying.  Each of these topics includes strategies and techniques to help readers get unstuck and take their lives to the next level.

     Unique coverage is designed to help readers:

  • Increase their energy by plugging their “energy leaks”
  • Strengthen their intuition—that gut feeling where you “just know”
  • Avoid regrets by using one simple technique
  • Focus their intention and do what they came here to do

In addition to the 45 activities, this book includes one graph, six appendices, 97 endnotes, and an index. It is categorized as motivational and inspirational.
