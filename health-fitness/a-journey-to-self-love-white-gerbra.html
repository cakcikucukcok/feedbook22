A Journey to Self-Love is a love story about a very sensitive girl, Sylvia, who grew up feeling lost and unloved by her parents, while also struggling to cope with their abusive relationship at home. In her teenage years, she meets Lesley, the love of her life, who soon just seems to mirror back how she really feels about love and herself. Her life then turns into a rollercoaster of mixed emotions, love, adultery, and near-death experiences. Read how she grew strong from a life where at some point, death seemed as her only option. This book is full of life lessons from growing out of abusive relationships into stronger self-confidence, inner peace, and self-love, all based on a true story.
