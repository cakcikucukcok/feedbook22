Life gets tough, obstacles seem insurmountable and a little bit of encouragement can go a long way.



For over 30 years, the Honourable D. Neletha Butterfield, a former Cabinet Minister for the Bermuda Government, has been a messenger of motivation, enrichment and empowerment.  A prolific speaker, Butterfield’s thought provoking talks have lifted minds and spirits in women’s groups,  prisons,  schools and international assemblies to name a few. Her unique style keeps audiences engaged and inspired–long after her last word. 



For the first time, a collection of Butterfield’s most powerful speeches has been compiled to push you to new heights.  Speeches for the Soul also serves as a coursebook for new motivational speakers and a rich source of documentary history for those interested in the lives of people of the African Diaspora.  



A true gem, Butterfield has laid a solid foundation for future generations to know that from which they come.
