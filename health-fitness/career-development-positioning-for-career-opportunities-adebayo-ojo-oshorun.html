Career Development significantly determines the nature and quality of individuals' lives; the kind of people they become, the people they meet, the sense of purpose they have and the income at their disposal. it equally determines the contribution they make to their families, communities, the societies they are part of and their nations. It also determines the rate of growth and development of a nation as well as her prosperity, hence, the conception of this book. The book is meant to educate people on how they can get career and business opportunities and grab them through academic excellence, diligence and determination. it equally shows the reality of our nation's industralisation and what should be done to generate more jobs as well as eradicate poverty. it shows people how they can form business, raise capital and make the business grow. It informs people the effect of laziness and procrastination as a hindrance to career success. This book will be helpful to students, corps members, job seekers....
