What makes 40 Days to the BRAVE New You so different is it is all about you! This book concentrates on finding your authentic self. It explores your identity and personal reality. You get to demonstrate your own value and significance. You will learn how to love you unconditionally. It is time to train yourself to be kind to you.  

 

Quit waiting for someone else to give you permission to live. 

 

It is time to kick fear and let it be under your feet. Come on a journey to get to know the most important friend you will ever have besides God – YOU!  

 

Your future does not have to look like your past. 

You can be BRAVE. 

 

Become BOLD in living, RESPECTFUL toward yourself, ACTIVE in big and small steps, VICTORIOUS over your past and ENCOURAGING for yourself, and encounter LOVE without limits for yourself.
