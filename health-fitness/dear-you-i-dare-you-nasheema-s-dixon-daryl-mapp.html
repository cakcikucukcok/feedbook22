Dear You, Dare You is a collection of poetry that tells a story relatable to young women and single mothers around the world. Young women often go through circumstances that they feel like they are the only ones who encounter them. Dear You,Dare You has a message of empowerment and encouragement to all those who may come across it.  With an total of of 49 poems they are broken down into 10 chapters that each dare you to take forward movement in life's journey and challenges. Even if the reader isn’t a woman it will still uplift you and help you encourage and connect with the women in your life.
