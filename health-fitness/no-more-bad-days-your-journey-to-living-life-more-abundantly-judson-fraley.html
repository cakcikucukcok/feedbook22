You were not created to live in mediocrity or in subpar circumstances. According to God’s Word, we are to have life and have it more abundantly. Your finances, health and relationships are all designed to serve your highest good. All you have to do is want it, and it’s yours. 

No More Bad Days proves how your ability to manifest the life that you dream of relies on how you think and what you do. Judson Fraley shares 15 principles that will guide you in your transformational journey to living more abundantly. Principle 1 declares that you deserve to live, love and enjoy your life. Deciding to pursue your best life and taking full responsibility in the pursuit of it are key, however. 

The journey isn’t easy, but it’s worth it. If you believe that you can do it and know that you deserve it, then join Judson, theLIFEspeaker, in affirming, loving and celebrating you!
