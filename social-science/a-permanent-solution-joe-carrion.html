There is nothing like getting your information first hand. Back in 1972, I unexpectedly found myself in Castros Cuba where I spent four days, before being allowed to come home. While there, I had a chance to hear in some detail from a government official about the campaign they had in place against the U.S.

As can be expected, I was skeptical, and not that concerned, about their having any hope of success. But, over the years, an objective look at the course of events in our country has made me aware of disturbing changes and trends that indicate that we are heading in the wrong direction. We seem to be abandoning all the basic principles that made us the greatest country in the world. A course correction is not going to be easy, but to do nothing is to surrender. We must act now!
